About

Hi! I'm currently a Junior at Canyon Crest Academy in San Diego, California. I'm very passionate about programming, technology, entrepreneurship, business, photo and video editing, and leadership!

I've been fascinated by computer science ever since I was in 3rd grade, when I discovered that instead of playing games, I could create my own using a website called Scratch. Ever since then, I've loved building tools for a variety of applications, especially web development and machine learning! I've also been involved in many leadership opportunities through Scouting ever since 5th grade, and I've loved photo and video editing ever since I've been in 6th grade.
